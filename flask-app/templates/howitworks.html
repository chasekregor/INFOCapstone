MoviRec

How It Works

MoviRec uses a machine learning algorithm called k-means clustering.

k-means clustering is used to create groups of things based on their attributes or preferences.

MoviRec was then trained on the MovieLens 20 Million Dataset.

In doing so, it "learned" over 100k ratings and all their various groups.

Then, based on the movies you rated, you're assigned to a group.

MoviRec then simply recommends movies that best represent that group.
