Introduction:

MovieRec tries to predict movies you may like.

MovieRec gives you two sets of recommendations:

  • A set based on the movies you liked.
  • Another set based on the movies people similar to you liked as well.
The more ratings you give MovieRec the more accurate your predictions will be.
